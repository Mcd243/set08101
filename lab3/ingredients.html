Ingrediants

  • butter, for the tin
  • 800g soft cheese
  • 225g caster sugar
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 200g soured cream
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
If you want a really nice cheesecake pick the best ingredients you can find .

Recipe tips: On the side

ROASTED APRICOTS Cut some apricots in half, toss with a little honey and a splash of Vin Santo or Spanish sherry for a boozy kick and roast at 200C/180C fan/gas 6 for 15 mins.

MACERATED BERRIES Steep some strawberries and raspberries in a little sugar and lemon juice. The acidity helps balance out the rich cheesecake.
